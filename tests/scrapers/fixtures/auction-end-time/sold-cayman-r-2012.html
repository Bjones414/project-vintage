This Porsche 987 Cayman got away, but there are more like it here.

2012 Porsche Cayman R

USA
N No Reserve
Sold for USD $68,000 on 4/27/2677Comments
View Result
MakePorsche
ModelPorsche 987 Cayman
Era2010s
OriginGerman
LocationLocated in United States

This 2012 Porsche Cayman R is finished in Peridot Metallic over Black leather and is powered by a 3.4-liter flat-six paired with a seven-speed PDK transaxle and a limited-slip differential. Equipment includes body-color-painted Sport seat shells and interior accents as well as a fixed rear wing, a SportDesign steering wheel, Black 19″ lightweight wheels, a Bose surround-sound system, and PCM with navigation. The seller purchased the car in April 2024 and has since added approximately 4k of the 65k indicated miles. This 987.2 Cayman R is now offered at no reserve with a Porsche certificate of authenticity, manufacturer’s literature, a battery tender, service records, two keys, a clean Carfax report, and a clean California title in the seller’s name.

The Cayman R was approximately 120 pounds lighter than the S model due to a fixed rear wing, aluminum doors, and additional lightweight components. This example is finished in Peridot Metallic (2S1) with R-specific graphics and features bi-xenon headlights with dynamic cornering lights as well as quarter-panel intakes and dual center exhaust outlets. A ceramic coating has been applied, and blemishes are photographed in the gallery.

Black-painted 19″ lightweight wheels are mounted with Michelin Pilot Sport All Season 4 tires measuring 235/35 up front and 265/35 out back. The car is equipped with Porsche Stability Management (PSM), and braking is handled by red four-piston Porsche-branded calipers over cross-drilled rotors.

Black leather trims the Sport seats, which feature body-color-painted shells. Body-color trim also adorns the dashboard and center console, and other appointments include Porsche Communication Management (PCM) with navigation as well as a Bose surround-sound system, Bluetooth connectivity, automatic climate control, cruise control, and red seatbelts and door pulls.

Sport and Sport Plus buttons and associated programming were installed by Harper Porsche in October 2021.

The leather-wrapped SportDesign steering wheel is equipped with shift paddles and frames a hoodless instrument cluster housing factory instrumentation. The digital odometer indicates 65k miles, approximately 4k of which were added under current ownership.

The mid-mounted 3.4L flat-six is located ahead of the rear cargo area and was factory rated at 330 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. Records indicate that the serpentine belt was replaced in September 2022 and the oil was changed in March 2026.

Power is sent to the rear wheels via a seven-speed PDK dual-clutch automatic transaxle and a limited-slip differential. Work in May 2023 included replacing the transaxle fluid pan, filter, and gasket.

A Porsche Certificate of Authenticity, a battery tender, manufacturer’s literature, service records, and two keys will accompany the car.

The Carfax report is free of accidents or other reported damage.
