This Porsche 987 Cayman got away, but there are more like it here.

2009 Porsche Cayman 6-Speed

USA
Sold for USD $42,000 on 4/24/2664Comments
View Result
MakePorsche
ModelPorsche 987 Cayman
Era2000s
OriginGerman
LocationLocated in United States

This 2009 Porsche Cayman was purchased new by owner, and it has 13k miles. The car has been registered in the Carolinas from new. It is finished in Arctic Silver Metallic over black leather and is powered by a 2.9-liter flat-six paired with a six-speed manual transaxle. Equipment includes a Fabspeed exhaust system, a deployable rear spoiler, fog lights, 17″ Cayman II wheels, heated and ventilated seats, automatic climate control, and cruise control. This 987.2 Cayman is now offered by the seller on behalf of the owner with a clean Carfax report and a clean North Carolina title.

The car is finished in Arctic Silver Metallic (92U) and features fog lights, quarter-panel intakes, a speed-dependent rear spoiler, and twin exhaust outlets.

The 17″ Cayman II wheels are mounted with Michelin tires. The car is equipped with Porsche Stability Management, and braking is handled by four-piston Porsche-branded calipers over cross-drilled rotors. The brake fluid was flushed in March 2026.

The cabin features heated and ventilated seats upholstered in black leather along with a color-coordinated dashboard and carpets. Amenities include a CD stereo, automatic climate control, auto-dimming mirrors, and cruise control.

The leather-wrapped steering wheel frames a central 8k-rpm tachometer, a 175-mph speedometer, and gauges for fuel level and coolant temperature. The digital odometer indicates 13k miles.

The mid-mounted 2.9-liter flat-six is located ahead of the rear cargo area and was factory rated at 265 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, which is sent to the rear wheels via a six-speed manual transaxle. The seller tells us that a Fabspeed exhaust system has been installed. The oil was changed and the battery was replaced in March 2026.

The Carfax report is free of accidents or other reported damage.
