## 911 Air-Cooled Era

- No US-market Carrera RS 2.7 was built. The US market received the Carrera RS (IMSA version) and later the Carrera RSR — different cars.

## Research Notes and Known Gaps

### Figures requiring priority verification before publication:

1. **993 Turbo S** — 345 (community consensus) vs. 435 (Porsche Newsroom). Strongly recommend verifying against Porsche AG or Patrick Paternie's book.
2. **993 GT2** — Total and breakdown figures vary materially across sources (57 street-only per one source; 161+33 per another; 194 total street per a third). The competition cars further complicate the count.
3. **964 total production** — Three commonly cited totals: 62,172; 63,762; 66,571. The spread is large enough to matter. The 964uk.com PDF document may have the most granular factory data.
4. **991.2 GT2 RS** — 1,000 (official) vs. ~1,580 (enthusiast registry analysis). Both may be correct if counting production authorization vs. actual deliveries.
5. **997.1 GT3 RS** — 1,106 vs. 1,168 vs. 1,909. The highest figure likely includes a different production year scope.
6. **944 Turbo S** — 775 vs. ~1,300 vs. 1,635. The variation reflects different spec definitions.
7. **968 total production** — 11,763 (968 Register) vs. 12,776 (general automotive press). The register is likely more reliable.

### Data not found / marked [UNVERIFIED]:
- 911 SC (1978–1983) individual year production totals
- 987 Boxster and Cayman complete worldwide totals by variant
- 718/982 generation total production
- 718 GT4 RS total worldwide production (official)
- 992 GT3 RS final worldwide total (production ongoing)
- 930 Turbo 3.0-liter complete year-by-year US/RoW breakdown
- 993 Speedster — only 2 documented examples

### Recommended additional research:
- Adrian Streather's "Porsche 911 and Derivatives" reference series — most cited source for variant-level production data
- Patrick Paternie, *The Porsche 911 Red Book* — standard reference for air-cooled production counts
- Porsche AG press kit archive (newsroom.porsche.com) — generation launch press kits often contain total production figures at model end
- porschehangout.com/porsche-tools/porsche-production-numbers — aggregates factory data; worth cross-checking
- stuttcars.com — the most consistently sourced online database for model-by-model breakdowns; used extensively in this document
